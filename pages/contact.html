Where to find us

Welcome to our store located in the vibrant city of Rosario, Argentina! Finding us is easy, and we can't wait to welcome you. Here's a simple guide on how to reach our shop:

  • By Car:

    If you're driving, simply enter our address into your GPS or preferred navigation app. There is convenient parking available near our shop for your convenience.

  • Public Transportation:

    Rosario has a well-connected public transportation system. You can reach us by bus or taxi. Feel free to ask the driver to drop you off at our location, and you'll find us just a short walk away.

  • On Foot:

    If you're exploring the city on foot, our shop is situated in a prime location, easily accessible from popular landmarks.
