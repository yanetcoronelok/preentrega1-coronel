About us

Our team, our history

Sally Bee was born as a catalyst for dreams that have led us to travel new directions and portray everything that crosses our path.

Today, the course of this adventure is marked by those who want to be part of this work team and by those who want to make us part of their lives to immortalize THEIR OWN ADVENTURE.

With its founder known as Sally Bee, the venture adopted her pseudonym for greater and better recognition. Today Sally Bee is much more than a person, she is a team of dreamers who, like bees, symbols of hard work, a strong work ethic and teamwork, strive to grow and put their best into each project.

What motivates us?

We are convinced that life can be a wonderful adventure if we are willing to pursue our dreams. It is important for us to convey that everything is achievable if we make the effort. This is why our principles are closely linked to the words of Will Durant and Pablo Picasso.

“Excellence is an art won by training and habituation. We do not act rightly because we have virtue or excellence, but we rather have those because we have acted rightly. We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act but a habit.”

Will Durant

“Inspiration exists, but it has to find you working.”

Pablo Picasso

Our values

Never stop learning

We believe that in this modern, fast-paced world, staying humble is the key to growth. That’s why we strive to stay curious and are always eager to learn.

Nurture that team spirit

We may be strong as individuals, but together we are a force. Collaboration is key among the team and with our clients.

Own it

Obstacles, opportunities, and new situations are a challenge to embrace. A good leader owns the responsibility to inspire and motivate co-workers.

Make an impact

We are here to make a difference—to our clients and to our community. When people feel empowered, they’re more inspired to do their best work.

Rosario, Argentina
